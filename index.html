Our Team

Gordon Ramsay

Gordon James Ramsay OBE is a British chef, restaurateur, writer,and television personality .He was born in Johnstone, Scotland, and raised in Stratford-upon-Avon, England .His restaurants have been awarded 16 Michelin stars in total and currently holds a total of seven Ramsay is known for his bluntness, as well as occasionally fiery temper, strict demeanour, and frequent use of expletives

Burak Özdemir

Burak Özdemir, nicknamed CZNBurak, is a Turkish chef and restaurateur. He owns the Hatay Medeniyetler Sofrasi chain of restaurants, which consist of three branches; Taksim, Aksaray and Etiler Burak Ozdemir, known on social media as CZN Burak, was born on March 24, 1994 in the Hatay province of Turkey. The chef is of Syrian-Arab descent

Alan Wong

Alan Wong’s 1857 King Street Honolulu, HI 96826 (808) 949-2526
Alan Wong is considered one of the godfathers of modern Hawaiian cuisine. He was part of the group of on-island chefs who, in the early 1990s, decided to erase the poor-food reputation in Hawaii and replace it with innovative, sustainable riffs on local favorites
